FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Gatherly. Can't find your answer? Chat with us.

What is Gatherly?+
Gatherly is an all-in-one platform for building and managing communities and events. It combines community management tools, event creation, member engagement features, and analytics — all in a single, beautifully designed product. Whether you run a student club, a professional network, or a large organization, Gatherly makes it easy to grow and manage your community.
Can I create unlimited events?+
On the free Starter plan, you can create up to 5 events per month. With our Pro plan ($12/month), you can create unlimited events of any type — from small meetups to large conferences. Enterprise plans also include unlimited events with additional features like custom registration forms and attendee data exports.
Is Gatherly free to use?+
Yes! Gatherly offers a free Starter plan that includes up to 100 members, basic community management tools, and up to 5 events per month. You can use Gatherly for free indefinitely. When your community grows and needs more features, you can upgrade to our Pro or Enterprise plans anytime.
Can organizations and businesses use Gatherly?+
Absolutely. Gatherly is built for organizations of all sizes — from small nonprofits and student clubs to large enterprises. Our Enterprise plan includes custom integrations, SSO, advanced permissions, dedicated support, and white-label options tailored to organizational needs. Contact our sales team to learn more about our Enterprise offerings.
Is there mobile support?+
Yes! Gatherly is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile. We also have native iOS and Android apps currently in beta, which offer push notifications, quick event check-ins, and an optimized mobile community experience. Sign up and join the waitlist to get early access.
